## Project Description
Plant Care Assistant is an innovative application designed to help users take better care of their plants. The app provides personalized care recommendations, watering schedules, and plant identification features to ensure healthy plant growth.

## Key Features
- Plant identification through image recognition
- Personalized care schedules and reminders
- Detailed plant care guides and tips
